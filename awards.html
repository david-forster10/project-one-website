National Apprenticeship Awards

The National Apprenticeship Awards are provided by the government for both employers and apprentices and are the most prestigious award set for UK apprenticeships. We have had our apprentices place within the 'Top 100 Apprentices' category as well as several of their employers achieve awards from the 'Top 100 Employers' category.

Ofsted Outstanding Training Provider

As a training provider, we have achieved one of, if not, the highest level of award for any education body. The Ofsted Outstanding award shows our tutors' commitment to providing, and maintaining, a high level of education in all areas of business. We have consistently achieved outstanding for 4 years in a row and make it our goal to maintain this legacy.

Ofordshire Apprenticeship Awards

This was held in Oxfordshire in March, gaining the support of NatWest, Barclays as well as multiple other high profile businesses. Several of our apprentices participated this year, winning awards across a multitude of fields. First Choice too were highly commended as a runner up in the 'Training Provider Awards', an impressive feat for going up against such fierce competition.

Asian Apprenticeship Awards

The Asian Apprenticeship Awards are an event to showcase the talent within the BME Asian community, and the employers who are reaching out to train them. This year we were able to win the award for training provider of the year, a fantastic result that shows our commitment to transforming the lives of young people across the UK. Furthermore, one of our apprentices, Lubna Suleman won the award in the category of Creative and Digital.

Warwickshire and Coventry Awards

In the Midlands, we were also able to win an award for training provider of the year from the Coventry and Warwickshire award scheme. The competition for this was incredibly competitive with companies such as Coventry City Council and the Warwickshire College group competing.
